About Varsity Plug

Our Mission

At Varsity Plug, we're dedicated to simplifying the university application process for South African students. We understand that applying to universities can be overwhelming, which is why we've created a platform that makes it easier, faster, and more accessible.

  • Easy university applications
  • APS score calculations
